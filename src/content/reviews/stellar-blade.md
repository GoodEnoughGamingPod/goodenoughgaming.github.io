Stellar Blade arrived with massive hype and some equally massive expectations. Does Shift Up's action epic deliver? Mostly.

## Combat

This is where Stellar Blade shines brightest. The combat system is tight, responsive, and deeply satisfying once you master its parry timing. Boss fights are a particular highlight — each one feels like a genuine test of skill.

## The World

Post-apocalyptic Earth looks stunning here. The environmental design ranges from desolate wastelands to neon-lit urban ruins, and it's all rendered beautifully on PS5. Exploration is rewarding, with plenty of hidden gear and lore to find.

## Story

Unfortunately, the narrative doesn't quite stick the landing. The setup is intriguing, but the pacing stumbles in the middle act and some character motivations feel underdeveloped. It's serviceable, but you're here for the gameplay.

## Performance

Rock-solid 60fps in performance mode with minimal hitches. The game looks and feels great in motion.

## Verdict

Stellar Blade is a strong action game with excellent combat held back by a middling story. If you're looking for a challenging, stylish brawler on PS5, it's well worth your time.

**Rating: 7/10**
